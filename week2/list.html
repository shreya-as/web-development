Steps to Prepare a Cup of Tea

  1. Boil water.
  2. Add tea leaves or a tea bag.
  3. Let it steep for 3-5 minutes.
  4. Add sugar and milk (optional).
  5. Pour the tea into a cup and enjoy.

Benefits of Drinking Tea

  • Helps reduce stress.
  • Boosts immunity.
  • Improves digestion.
  • Contains antioxidants.
  • Provides a refreshing feeling.
